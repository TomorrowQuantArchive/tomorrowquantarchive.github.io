01

What is this?

The successor to the IMC Prosperity guide Ctrl+Alt+DefeatTheMarket and the KnowledgeBase built for student maths, statistics, actuarial, and quant finance groups. The aim is to keep the material free, impartial, non-commercial, and useful.

02

Why does it exist?

It exists because a lot of useful quant knowledge is scattered, hidden behind status games, or only discovered after wasting months. This archive turns that accumulated effort into stepping stones: if we cannot all cross the river easily, we can at least build a bridge.

03

How should I read it?

Start where you are. From “I have no clue”, move towards “I know what quant is”, then towards “I know what I want to build”, and eventually towards “I know what strategy fits this regime”. The jokes are not gone; they have merely been risk-managed.
