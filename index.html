About this archive

This site collects guides, notes, code explanations, and research writeups for people learning quantitative trading through competitions and self-directed projects.

What will be included

  • IMC Prosperity-style strategy guides
  • Market making and order book notes
  • Backtesting and research templates
  • Statistical arbitrage examples
